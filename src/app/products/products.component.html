Welcome to Fiber & Kraft!

For fiber enthusiasts who want to buy custom blend wool and hand dyed yarn, Fiber & Kraft curates and sells fiber for spinning, knitting and crocheting.

Our products are made from high quality wool that's ethically sourced from midwest shepherds and milled locally in Minnesota. Unlike mills producing large volumes of wool from around the world, each skein of yarn or bump of fiber can be matched to a flock, ram, ewe or lamb.
